To have a Gospel-centered mentality in all things, especially within the context of social and political issues, is often equated with apathy and carelessness. Unfortunately, there are way too many examples of Christians speaking the truth without love, those who's theology has not yet translated into daily action. But we cannot let these poor examples diminish our view of the power of the Word of God to transform lives. Any life. Any background. No matter how dark the soul, the Gospel is always capable of bringing light. We all must humble ourselves. We all must repent. And God is gracious to incline His ear to the suffering, the broken-hearted. Jesus Christ said, "my Kingdom is not of this world" not to discourage practical love, but emphasize the fact that our ultimate allegiance belongs to Him. Pray We All Repent is an effort to remember that, and to live it out.
